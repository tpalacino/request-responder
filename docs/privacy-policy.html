1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Request Responder browser extension (“the Service”) handles user information. The Service is intentionally designed to operate without collecting, storing, or processing any user data of any kind.

2. Information Not Collected

The Service does not collect, store, process, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No phone numbers
  • No IP addresses
  • No device identifiers
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No location data
  • No files, messages, or user‑generated content
  • No behavioral, preference, or profiling data

The Service functions entirely without requiring any user information, whether personally identifiable or anonymous.

3. Third‑Party Services

The Service does not integrate with third‑party analytics providers, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external data processors. No data is shared with any external parties because no data is collected in the first place.

4. Children’s Privacy

Because the Service does not collect any information from any user, it is, by design, aligned with privacy regulations concerning children (such as COPPA) to the extent they apply. No personal data from children or adults is collected, stored, or processed.

5. Changes to This Policy

If the Service is ever updated to include features that require data collection, this Privacy Policy will be revised before any such changes take effect. Any future version of the Service that collects data will clearly describe what is collected, why, and how it is handled.
